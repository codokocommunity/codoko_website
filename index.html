About Us

We are a student-run coding community, welcoming both students and like-minded individuals. We provide a range of offerings, including free and paid events, articles, notes, and much more!

We Are Codoko Community

Codoko Community is a group for students and folks who love coding. Here, you can team up on projects and work together. We've got plenty to offer.

Our community officially launched on October 15, 2023, thanks to two students from ABSSIT in Meerut, affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University in Lucknow. Jatin Kumar Mehta is the Founder and current Chairman, while Aryan Shakya is the Co-founder.
